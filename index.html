FoodDoof website is here to help you find an event to have fun or a restaurant to enjoy a beautiful evening!

The perfect app to plan the rest of your night out! Get off the couch and socialize! This app will allow you to pick a restaurant and will list available events nearby or vice-versa.

  • The target audience of this app is intended for those have either a restaurant or an event that they will attend in mind. It is meant to provide options for an activity before/after a meal OR it will provide restaurant options in proximity to an event the user will attend.
  • Pick a restaurant or event and based on the location, the app will provide supplemental ideas for a night out (food options/activities).
